Perfect Dodge

Infinite-Dodging Hyper-Casual game with Neon-Aesthetic

Number of players : 1
Age : 3+
Game Lenght : 2-3min

Swipe across your screen to get as far as possible in Perfect Dodge. Along the way, you'll encounter many different and increasingly dangerous enemies, as well as power-ups to help you cope with the dangers.

Perfect Dodge was created during the second year of Game Design at Isart DIGITAL Paris. It was made in 2 months in collaboration with 2nd year Game Design-Programmers.
