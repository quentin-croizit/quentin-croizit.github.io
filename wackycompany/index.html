Wacky Company

3 Person, 1 Tank, 1 Custom-Controller made with a Makey-Makey

Number of players : 3
Age : 16+
Game Lenght : 10min

Wacky Company needs 3 people to be played. Each one has a specific chore in the tank.

The first one is controlling the tank itself, using two levers to manoeuvre each track.
Another person is controlling the turret, he has to turn the turret (independently from the tank), he can also reload and shoot shells.
Both of them have a view of the terrain on their respective screens.
The last one has a very special place, he has a map of the terrain and is in charge of the radio. Two channels are available to him at all time: ally and enemy.
On the allied line the commander will tell him information about what's happening and your objectives, meanwhile, on the enemy line, information about their strategy are given.

This game was made during the second year of Game Design at Isart DIGITAL Paris. It was made in 2 months by 5 Game Designers.
