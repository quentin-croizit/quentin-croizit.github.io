ToyBox

Help Didou your plush friend, in this Visual Novel made for children

Number of players : 1
Age : 3+
Game Lenght : 10min

Toybox is an interactive story in the form of a visual novel made for children from the age of 5 to 8. Help Didou, your best friend, retrieve his button that Captain Button stole him while weren't there!

Toybox was created during the second year of Game Design at Isart DIGITAL Paris. It was made in 5 weeks in collaboration with 2nd year Game Artists.
