The Cake is Alive

Stealth game where you sneak out of your room to get the some cake

Number of players : 1
Age : 7+
Game Lenght : 15min

In The Cake is Alive, you play a punished child who has gone to bed without having had dessert. In order to have some, you will have to infiltrate your house, but watch out for your parents! Their hearing is infallible ...

The Cake is Alive is an infiltration game relying heavily on sound, the player has the possibility to interact with a large part of the home furniture in order to create noise, allowing the control of their parents' movements.

This game was made during the preparatory year of Game Design at Isart DIGITAL Paris. It was made in 1 month in collaboration with game programmers.
