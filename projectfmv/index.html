Project FMV

Help your character through 4 small adventures

Number of players : 1
Age : 3+
Game Lenght : 5min

Our objective when making Project FMV was to try our hands at making that type of game. We wanted to innovate on the FMV concept and make something different. That's why we made and anthology of gameplays around the FMV. 4 gameplays were developed.

The first one, Maison Hant√©e, is based around constructing the environment room by room for your character to escape from a ghost.

The second one, the one I worked on, is called Filtres, and it utilizes the RGB components of the screen. The player can only see one of those channel at a time, making things a certain color appear or disappear depending on the active color channel.

In the third one, called Rewind, you can go back in time to get item from the past wher you need it.

Finally, in the fouth gameplay, Mouvement, you can rotate the couch your character is sitted onto and make them fall into 4 different directions to continue the story.

This game was made during the third year of Game Design at Isart DIGITAL Paris. It was made in 1 and a half month by 6 Game Designers.
