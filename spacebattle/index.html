Space Battle

One Button Game - Arena FFA for 2 to 8 players

Number of players : 2-8
Age : 7+
Game Lenght : 1min

Space Battle is a One Button Game in the arcade genre that is played locally and has a great nervousness. In this game, each player has only one button to turn and/or shoot.

The objective? Kill all your enemies. To achieve this, a wide variety of power-ups are available to the players, which prevents the game from becoming too repetitive.

With its artistic direction and its music, players are directly immersed in an atmosphere corresponding to the era of arcade games.

Space Battle was created during the preparatory year of Game Design at Isart DIGITAL Paris in 1 month as an individual project.
