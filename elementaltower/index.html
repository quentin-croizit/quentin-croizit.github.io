Elemental Tower

Use the power of the element to fight your ennemies in this brawler

Number of players : 1
Age : 13+
Game Lenght : 15min

In Elemental Tower, you play as an element knight, trying to fight its way through the Tower.

You can change the element you are using while fighting to create elemental combos and inflict more damages to your enemies!

This game was made during the preparatory year of Game Design at Isart DIGITAL Paris. It was made in 1 and a half month in collaboration with game programmers.
