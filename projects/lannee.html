Overview

In 2022, I started writing everyday. I wrote short stories of around 200 words. But this year was a little bit too full for me, and so I had to stop.

Fast-forward to 2023, and the bitter taste of having failed at this hasn't fade. So I decided to give it another try, and this time : no miss, no lazyness, no excuses. I wrote one short-story everyday.

No two stories are linked together. Each one was made in its own bubble. From Lovecraftian horror, to hard-science and noir fiction, I tried my hands at every genre. (Oh, and it's all written in French)
