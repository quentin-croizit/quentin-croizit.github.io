Overview

We created this game during the Ludum Dare 55 with Tom Falempin.

In this game, demons will come to you, asking of you to summon them (you don't want to know what'll happen if you don't).

They'll simply give you the rite of summoning you have to perform. Draw the correct cicle, place the right artefact, some candles and ta-da!

Creation Time

72 hours

Roles

Game Designer

The first concept was as twist on the "Papa's" series of flash games. The more the idea evolved, the more it morphed into this extremely-hard and very demeaning concept.

2D Artist

I was still working on my pixel art skills from the previous jam I did. This time I tried to make it more freeform and flowy instead of rectangular and slick.

Tech Artist

The previous months, I started trying new things with the URP in Unity. I took this jam as an opportunity to develop those skills further, and try this chalk-based visuals.

Sound Designer

An chiptune audio ambiance made sense with the aesthetics. A lot of time was spent on the music which is a simple melody that Tom reversed to make more "demonic" sounding.
