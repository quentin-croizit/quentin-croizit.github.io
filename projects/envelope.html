Overview

We created this game during the Ludum Dare 51 with Hugo Teyssier & Tom Falempin.

| You play as a mail sorter with no coffee break. Sort your mail asap because it won't stop coming in and if it piles up too much ... well you'd be fired.

But beware because how you have to sort the mail changes regularly and the company has 0 mistake policy.

Game Designer

We came up with the context by first discussing ideas. I then took the base concept and extended it, exploring possiblities & tuning it.

2D Artist
