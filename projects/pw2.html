Overview

After leaving my previous work, I immediately started working on this project that was always in the back of my mind for the last 4 years.

PW2 is a reinterpretation of an old game I worked, for which I still have the cards, but the rules are long forgotten. Out of frustration the need to create a better version of it came.

PW2 is a City-Building card game in which you place down buildings to create synergies and obtain Civilization Points. Effects are varied in this 100-card pack, and no 2 games are alike!

Roles

Game Design

It took me 5 versions and 2 months of iteration to find the right fun/strategy balance. Making the game simple yet giving it depth is a great challenge.

Playtest

I organized playtests during events to confront the game to the public. Reading the feedbacks and implementing the results was a great deal of work.

2D Artist

Many of the icons I use come from game-icons.net. But when I can't find what I'm looking for, I use Figma to mash together or create from scratch the icons I need.
