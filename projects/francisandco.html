Overview

In this RPG, you play as three characters : Jess, Lisa & Ben. Get around San Fransico in your van, discover its most iconic neighborhoods and places. You can also Interact with the locals, get to know them and their story, help them by doing quests.

Fight the 2 different gangs in skateboard battles. Buy equipment, find info about them to strenghen your strategy and use your best tricks to beat them.

Roles

Narrative Design

I was in charge of creating the world, its history and the characters.
To create something believable I researched the history of San Francisco to be as accurate as possible in our depiction of the city.

Writing

Helped by native english speakers, I wrote all the dialogues for this game.
I used idioms and word choice to develop the characters and create more interesring interactions.

Game Design

I was part of the design team with 5 other Game Designers.
We worked with the constraints of the exercise to create something new and fun.

Balancing

Regarding the fighting part of the game, I helped with the balancing of the moves, their effects and the characters.
