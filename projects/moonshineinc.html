Overview

Take on the role of a young moonshiner, who's eager to show what's he capable of!

Mix your ingredients and create flavorful alcools. Prosper throughout multiple regions, manage your stock, money but most importantly ... your reputation.

Deliver your moonshine to the illegal outlets that populate the map, and beware the police (they're onto you). From your hometown mountains, through the thickest forest and the muddiest swamp, conquer the big city and prove your worth!

Roles

Quest Designer

I was originally brought onboard to design the main and side quests. My objective was to always challenge the player to push them into discovering new things within the game mechanics. I also gave players more freedom with their playstyle by using choices in the event system.

Game Designer

I helped redesigning the main mixing mechanic and roeworked the technlogy points system to make it easier to understand. Redesiging the technology system, combined with the machines balancing and the quests gave me a great overview and allowed me to make more impactful decision.

Balancing

One of my other task was to tweak the machines to better reflect the progression of the player. It also allowed me to make the progression curve more interesting by introducing choices and trade-offs in the player purchases.
