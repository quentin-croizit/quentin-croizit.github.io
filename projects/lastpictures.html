Overview

We created this game during a friend-organized jam between the me and my friends over at Enter Space Game.

| Play as a detective who talks the ghosts that haunt the living. Check your mail, see a card, look at the photograph and go to the place.

Discover what's important to them by looking at the last picture taken of them. Use those informations when talking to them to help them in the afterlife.

Roles

Game Designer

We found the idea of helping demons very fast and that's the point were I started working on the flow of the game and it's structure.

Narrative Designer

I wrote the characters and came up with their backstories. This process made the puzzle design easier since I had elements to go from.

Puzzle Designer

After having the flow and the story, I was able to design the puzzle. I worked closely with the 2D Artist to place items in the photograph and to create 'false' items in the last phase.
